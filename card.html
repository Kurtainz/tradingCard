English Cocker Spaniel

A merry-natured dog breed that was originally bred to assist in hunting

  • Average lifespan: 12 years
  • Height: Female: 36–41 cm, Male: 38–43 cm
  • Weight: Female: 12–15 kg, Male: 13–16 kg
  • Temperament: Playful, Faithful, Friendly, Quiet, Affectionate, Trainable

The Cocker Spaniel is primarily a beloved companion dog breed, though he remains a capable bird dog at heart. Beautiful to look at (and labour-intensive to groom), the Cocker’s amenable, cheerful disposition also makes him a treat to have in the family.
